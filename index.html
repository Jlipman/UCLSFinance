About Us

The Finance Club is an organization at the University of Chicago Laboratory Schools that is dedicated to teaching students about the stock market and investing. It also provides an opportunity for students to trade stocks with a tangible portfolio and connect them with real world experts.



Board

The Finance Club is an entirely Student run organization. The club is led by dedicated student who seek to move the club forward.
